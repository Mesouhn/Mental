Mental Health

Mental health is a long-standing
issue that has not received
enough attention. A major reason
why mental health is not addressed
is that people associate mental
illness with weakness and hide it.
Some of the signs that someone
may be struggling with
mental health are: making
excuses to avoid activities,
withdrawing from social situations,
physically abusing themselves
or others, or constantly feeling on edge.

Mental health may be difficult to
understand and often goes undetected.
It may impact relationships
with family and friends.
It is critical for individuals
with depression
or anxiety to seek help
from a professional.
